SIBERIAN CAT

Siberian cats are a natural breed originating from Russia and are known for their robust, muscular build and luxurious triple-layered coat that helps them withstand cold climates. They have a friendly and playful temperament, making them excellent family pets. Siberians are highly intelligent, agile, and known for their agility and hunting skills. Their coat colors and patterns vary widely, but they typically have medium to large-sized bodies, round faces, and expressive eyes. The breed is hypoallergenic for many allergy sufferers due to its lower levels of allergenic proteins. Siberian cats have been cherished in Russian folklore for centuries and are now popular worldwide for their affectionate nature and striking appearance.

FACTS

  • COMMON NAME: SIBERIAN CAT
  • SCIENTIFIC NAME: FELIS CATUS
  • TYPE: MAMMALS
  • SIZE: MEDIUM TO LARGE (8 TO 17 POUNDS)
  • WEIGHT: 8 TO 17 POUNDS
  • AVERAGE LIFE SPAN: 12 TO 16 YEARS
