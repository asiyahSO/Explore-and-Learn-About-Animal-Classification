INTRODUCTION

Get ready to explore the amazing world of animals. This website is designed to make learning about animal classification interactive and exciting! Animals are organized into 5 main categories: mammals, birds, reptiles, amphibians, and fish. You can learn more about each animal by clicking on images to discover in-depth facts, behaviors, habitats, and special features of each species.

To make learning even more engaging, enjoy interactive quizzes and educational videos that bring animal life to your screen. Whether you're a student, teacher, or just curious about animal categories, this website will guide you to discover the wonders of wildlife in a fun and meaningful way.
