MAMMALS

Mammals are a group of warm-blooded animals known for having backbones, hair or fur, and the ability to feed their young with milk produced by special glands called mammary glands. They are among the most intelligent animals on Earth and can live in nearly every environment from oceans to deserts, forests to mountains. Mammals include a wide variety of species such as humans, elephants, tigers, bears, and even tiny creatures like bats and mice.
